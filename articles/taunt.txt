A move intentionally designed to provoke, annoy, disrespect, or mock opponents. It can be performed by pressing d-pad up. While taunts can sometimes be used to please the crowd and therefore provide a moral boost to the one taunting, some taunts are not well used and will have the opposite effect (see: SFAT)
